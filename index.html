03 — The Method 12 WEEKS, DOCUMENTED

We don't promise a new you. We build a stronger version of this one.

Every member runs the same twelve-week arc: assess, build, load, retest. Nothing mystical — just progressive tension, applied consistently, measured honestly.

Weeks 01–03 A

Assess & align

Movement screen, baseline strength tests, breath and bracing. We find the leaks before we add load.

Weeks 04–06 B

Build capacity

Volume rises. Positions harden. The reformer teaches your nervous system what control feels like.

Weeks 07–10 C

Load with intent

Heavier springs, heavier bars. Strength arrives quietly — stairs, luggage, posture, sleep.

Weeks 11–12 D

Retest & prove

Same tests, same conditions, new numbers. Your progress in ink, not in adjectives.
